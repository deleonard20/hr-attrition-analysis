## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition at PT. TransitIQ, 
a logistics and transportation company specializing in 
oversized goods delivery. With a current attrition rate 
of 16% — 6% above the healthy industry benchmark of 10% 
— the company is losing an estimated **Rp 4.740.000.000 
annually** in recruitment costs.

This analysis identifies the root causes of high attrition 
and delivers actionable recommendations projected to reduce 
attrition from **16% to 10% within 6 months**, 
potentially saving **Rp 1.800.000.000 annually**.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

TransitIQ is experiencing an employee attrition rate of 16%, 
resulting in high recruitment costs and operational disruption. 
Stakeholders require data-driven insights to identify root 
causes and implement targeted retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 SMART Objective

To identify the key drivers of employee attrition and provide 
actionable recommendations to reduce the attrition rate from 
**16% to 10% within 6 months**, measured through a real-time 
Power BI monitoring dashboard.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

| # | Finding | Attrition Rate |
|---|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Employees who work overtime | 31% (3x higher than non-overtime) |
| 2 | Sales Department | 21% (highest across departments) |
| 3 | Sales Representative role | 40% (highest across all job roles) |
| 4 | Early-tenure employees (Year 0–1) | 36% (3x higher than Year 5+) |

---

## 💡 Recommendations

**1. Reduce Overtime Workload**
- Conduct workload audit using RACI framework per role
- Identify and eliminate non-essential or automatable tasks
- Map workforce distribution to prevent understaffing

**2. Address Sales Department Issues**
- Restructure sales targets to be realistic and achievable
- Clarify job responsibilities for Sales Representatives
- Implement regular 1-on-1 check-ins to detect burnout early

**3. Improve Early Tenure Experience**
- Conduct exit interviews to validate root cause hypothesis
- Implement realistic job preview during recruitment
- Apply 30-60-90 day onboarding with gradual workload ramp-up
- Introduce mentor-mentee system for first 90 days
